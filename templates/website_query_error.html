Hi, I'm KorMu!

KorMu (Korean Music) was born from a desire to share the world of Korean "pop" music with others. While BTS may dominate the headlines and billboards, there are plenty of other artists who are making amazing music that also deserve to be heard. KorMu's recommendations are currently generated by using an unsupervised clustering algorithm that utilizes the audio features provided by Spotify. To use KorMu, enter the link of a track from Spotify that you like and KorMu will recommend you a list of songs that it think you might like.
